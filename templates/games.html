Game Tips

  • Guess the Number: Use a binary search approach — always halve your range.
  • Sudoku: Every row, column, and box must have 1–9 exactly once. Start with the easiest numbers first.
  • Palindromes: Famous palindromes: “madam”, “racecar”, “level”.
